How Teesra works

Every story, cut three ways.

01 ⚖️

The Facts

What actually happened

Claude AI reads every source, extracts only what's verified, and cross-references across outlets. A story doesn't appear unless 3+ credible publishers agree on the core facts. No spin. No filler.

02 🔵

Left Lens

How the left frames it

The Wire, Scroll, The Hindu, Indian Express — their framing, their emphasis, their concerns. Written in the authentic voice of progressive Indian media, not a description of it.

03 🔴

Right Lens

How the right frames it

OpIndia, Swarajya, Republic, Times Now — their narrative, their priorities, their reading of events. Same story, completely different emphasis. See the gap yourself.

India has a news problem.
Not a shortage of it.

We grew up on Twitter, Instagram, and YouTube. We watched the same event get reported ten different ways depending on who you followed. We learned, sometimes the hard way, that every headline has an agenda.

Teesra is the tool we wished existed. Not another news app that shoves breaking news at you. A thinking tool — one that shows you the facts, then shows you how the left sees it, how the right sees it, and what people on the street are actually saying.

01

Verified before visible

3+ sources must cover a story before it appears. We never break news. We get it right.

02

Show the lens, not the view

No editorial opinion. We show how others frame a story so you can see the frame.

03

Under 5 minutes, every morning

Dense, honest, fast. Read before your chai goes cold, then back to your life.
