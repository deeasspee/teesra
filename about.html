About Teesra

What is Teesra?

Teesra means third in Hindi.

In cricket, the teesra is a mystery delivery — it looks like one thing but does another. It deceives you into thinking you understand what is happening.

That is what most news does.

This product is named after the delivery that makes you look again.

Why we built this.

Indian news media has a fragmentation problem.

If you read only The Hindu, you see one India.
If you read only OpIndia, you see another.
Both are reading the same events. Neither is entirely wrong. Neither is entirely right.

Most Indians have settled into one media bubble without realising it. The algorithm feeds them more of what confirms what they already believe.

Teesra is the antidote.

How Teesra works.

✦ VERIFIED FACTS

Only what happened. No spin. Sourced from 3+ credible outlets before a story is selected.

◀ LEFT LENS

How progressive Indian media frames this story. Written in that voice, not about it.

▶ RIGHT LENS

How conservative Indian media frames this story. The same facts. A different emphasis.

● STREET PULSE

What specific groups of Indians are actually saying and feeling — not vague "public opinion" but named communities.

Built to be honest.

Every morning at 6:45 AM IST, an automated pipeline runs:

→

Fetches from 16+ news sources across the political spectrum

→

Scores each story for national relevance, youth relevance, and source diversity

→

Selects 20 stories that meet a 3-source minimum threshold

→

Sends each through Claude AI with strict prompt rules that prevent meta-language and manufactured opinion

→

Publishes to teesra.in and sends the newsletter before you wake up

No editor. No agenda. Just the brief.

20 stories daily · 16+ sources · 3 perspectives · Automated · Free forever (for now)
